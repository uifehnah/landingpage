About the Event

WCHSTT5K is a student run virtual 5k held over Strava. Over the course of 24 hours, students are challenged to run as many miles as they can with the benefit of supporting the charity. For every mile run, the charity will donate a dollar. Each mile is thusly rewarded with an ssl hour.

For a Good Cause

The charity supports national cancer research which is something we all support wholeheartedly. By participating in this event, you can have a positive impact on things like cancer research while also exercising.

Environmental

We have partnered with Natural Highways, with the goal of encouraging our fellow students to go outside and expore the natural world around them.

Social

Participate with your friends and compete to see who can raise the most money for charity.
